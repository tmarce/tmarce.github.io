Columbia City Bakery


Columbia City Bakery has been open for 10 years and offers a variety of pastries. We highly recommend the buttery croissants. If there was a tinder for food, we would swipe right.


Source: www.columbiacitybakery.com


Menu includes:

  • Croissants - Flaky, buttery, good for the soul.
  • Cookies - Sweet, tender, chocolatey, gives you acne.
  • Pies - Just like the ones your mother made. We would know.
  • Tarts - Smaller, meaner and grumpier version of a pie.
  • Cupcakes - Not cup shaped, but that's okay.
  • Puddings - Just can't stand up for themselves.
  • Danishes - We're not sure if these are actually Danish.
